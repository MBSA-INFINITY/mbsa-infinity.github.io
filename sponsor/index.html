Formed in 2007, we at Team BAJA NITK Racing aim of landing our institute flags in BAJA Racing events held annually by Mahindra. The competition raises a platform for teams from all over India to participate and showcase their hard work and dedication spent on their car over the entire year. We strive to promote our university, team, partners, and sponsors at the National and International levels. .

We are actively seeking sponsorship opportunities to help us achieve what we have always strived for - Engineering Endurance.
