TEAM BNR

ENGINEERING ENDURANCE

We at Baja NITK Racing or otherwise known as BNR, are an aggregate with some of the best minds amongst the budding engineers at National Institute of Technology Karnataka who are united by a common fiery passion for working synchronously and making the best all-terrain vehicle to compete with the annual Baja SAE Championship. Team BAJA NITK Racing was formed in 2007 and has grown over the years, with the curren team being 81 members strong.
